EDUCATION

I'm fourteen years old. So, I don't have a very long record in education. But, here goes nothing! I started attending school when I was three. My parents enrolled me into a pre-K on Washington Street in the City of Newburgh. Unfortunately, the school school has closed down, and I don't have much memory of it.

After graduating from Pre-K, I attended Meadow Hill for both elementary school and junior high. I spent nine years of my life there. Growing up, I attended bilingual classes up until the fifth grade. It was until then that I was placed into regular classes. In the beginning of junior high I wasn't doing well. But, I quickly cleaned up my act and got 80's and 90s all throughout seventh and eigth grade.

During the eight grade, I applied for the program called "Excelsior Academy". Thankfully so, I was accepted! So as soon as I graduated from Meadow Hill, I came here to Newburgh Free Academy North Campus, under the excelsior program. Right now, I am now a sophmore. I have a long way to go, but I'm excited!
